## TRADING CARD GAME SERVICES

### Trade-In Program

**How It Works:**
- Bring used Magic cards to counter
- Staff scans cards using TCGPlayer Pro app
- Instant market value assessment
- Offer 50% cash or 60% store credit

**What We Buy:**
- Magic: The Gathering (all sets, conditions)
- Pokemon (select cards, high-value only)
- Yu-Gi-Oh (select cards)

**Process:**
1. Customer presents cards at counter
2. Staff verifies condition (Near Mint, Lightly Played, etc.)
3. Staff scans cards, checks TCGPlayer mid price
4. Offer calculated (50% cash, 60% credit)
5. Customer accepts or declines
6. Transaction processed (cash paid or credit added to account)

8. Cards placed in display case or storage

**Transparency:**
- Show TCGPlayer price to customer

- No obligation to accept offer
